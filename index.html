HomeGrow Project

MECN 5055 Advanced Product Design

Project Overview

The HomeGrow project is a collaborative effort by students of MECN 5055 Advanced Product Design. Our goal is to design an innovative system that enables efficient and sustainable home gardening. This project integrates advanced design principles with practical applications in urban agriculture, focusing on accessibility, user experience, and environmental sustainability.

Design Features

Automated Watering System

This feature ensures that plants receive the optimal amount of water without manual intervention, leveraging soil moisture sensors and automated scheduling.

LED Growth Lighting

Our custom LED lighting system provides the necessary light spectrum for plant growth, adaptable to various plant types and growth stages.

Modular Design

The HomeGrow system's modular design allows for easy expansion and customization, accommodating spaces of different sizes and shapes.

Mobile App Integration

Users can monitor and control their HomeGrow system via a mobile app, which provides real-time data on plant health, system status, and environmental conditions.

Project Goals

Our primary goals with the HomeGrow project are to:

  • Enhance the efficiency of home food production.
  • Make sustainable gardening accessible to a wider audience.
  • Incorporate smart technology to simplify gardening tasks.
  • Teach valuable skills in product design and urban agriculture.
